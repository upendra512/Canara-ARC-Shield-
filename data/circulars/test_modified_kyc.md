**Amendment to Master Direction - Know Your Customer (KYC) Direction, 2016**

Please refer to the Master Direction - Know Your Customer (KYC) Direction, 2016, as amended from time to time. 

On a review of the operational difficulties faced by banks in physically verifying profiles downloaded from the Central KYC Registry (CKYCR), it has been decided to amend the instructions as under:

## Paragraph 65 - CKYC Registry Reliance
Banks downloading valid CIF profiles from the Central KYC Registry (CKYCR) are exempt from physical re-verification. Re-verification is not mandatory for these downloaded CKYC profiles, provided there is no mismatch in customer data.

These directions are issued under Section 35A of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 and Section 42 of the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934.
