**New Guidelines on NPA Provisioning Coverage Ratio**

Please refer to the prudential norms on Income Recognition, Asset Classification and Provisioning pertaining to Advances.

With a view to strengthening the balance sheets of commercial banks against potential credit shocks, the Reserve Bank has decided to mandate the following:

## Section 105 - NPA Provisioning Guidelines for Commercial Banks
Banks must maintain a provisioning coverage ratio of at least 70% for all non-performing assets (NPA) to ensure buffer safety. Credit risk assessments must be updated quarterly to evaluate exposure.

These guidelines shall come into effect from October 1, 2026.
