# RBI Master Direction - Foreign Exchange Management (FEMA) Guidelines

## Section 10 - Reporting of Liberalised Remittance Scheme (LRS)
All remittances made under the Liberalised Remittance Scheme (LRS) by resident individuals exceeding USD 250,000 per financial year require prior approval from the Reserve Bank of India. Transaction details and remittances must be reported in Form LRS on a monthly basis by the 10th of the succeeding month.

## Section 16 - Foreign Travel Card Limits
Authorised Dealers may issue foreign travel cards to resident individuals traveling abroad up to the limit of USD 250,000 or equivalent per financial year. Loading or reloading of travel cards beyond this limit must be blocked automatically by the card issuance system.

## Section 18 - External Commercial Borrowings (ECB) Reporting
Any External Commercial Borrowings (ECB) raised by eligible Indian entities must be reported to the Reserve Bank of India in Form ECB within 7 days from the end of the month in which the loan agreement is signed to obtain an LRN (Loan Registration Number).

## Section 25 - Export Proceeds Realisation
The realization and repatriation of export proceeds to India must be completed within 9 months from the date of export. For status holder exporters, the period of realization is 12 months from the date of export, unless extended by the Reserve Bank of India.
